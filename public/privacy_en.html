Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 14.06.2025

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and under what conditions we may share it with third parties.

1️⃣ Information We Collect

  • Your phone number: We use this to identify you and create a temporary account.
  • Telegram messages: We do not automatically fetch messages. We only retrieve messages from a chat after you explicitly select it in the app. We fetch up to 600 messages from that chat.
  • Analysis results: The summary of your conversation generated by DeepSeek is temporarily stored in our database for your future view.
  • Telegram session: We temporarily store your encrypted Telegram account credentials (session) for 8 days to enable this service. The phone number and the encrypted session may be kept in our database during this period.

2️⃣ How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and allow you to select conversations for analysis.
  • To fetch messages from your chosen conversations upon your explicit action.
  • To send those messages to DeepSeek for automated summarization.
  • To store the summary temporarily to show it back to you upon your request.

3️⃣ Information Sharing

We do not sell or transfer your phone number or messages to anyone except:

  • DeepSeek: We send messages you select to DeepSeek for analysis. We do not send phone numbers or account credentials — only the messages you chose.

4️⃣ Security

We implement reasonable measures to help protect your phone number, messages, and analysis results from unauthorized access.

5️⃣ Your Rights and Choices

  • Logout: At any time, you can terminate your session and delete all related data immediately.
  • Clear Summary: You can clear previously generated summaries from our database at any time.

6️⃣ Data Retention

  • Sessions expire after 8 days and are removed.
  • Summaries remain in the database until you clear them.

7️⃣ Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at analyt.tg@gmail.com.
